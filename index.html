ABOUT MALUSI MBOKAZI

Malusi Mbokazi is a South African gospel artist, performer, and mentor with a long and impactful career in the gospel music industry. Known for his powerful voice and inspiring ministry, he has performed on many prestigious stages and collaborated with leading gospel artists across Africa. Throughout his career, Malusi has appeared on major media platforms including interviews on Ukhozi FM, Vuma FM, and Igagasi FM, as well as television appearances on shows such as Adam on Tswane TV. His music has been played on various radio stations across the continent.

Malusi has performed at major gospel events including the All White Gospel Show at Carnival City, Moshito Gospel Festival, and several Easter festivals such as Kufezwe and Uvukile. He has also shared stages with well-known African gospel artists and participated in initiatives promoting unity across Africa.

Beyond performing, Malusi has contributed to developing new talent. From 2016 to 2020 he served as a judge and coach for the eThekwini Municipality Gospel Music Discovery Competition, helping discover and mentor emerging gospel singers. His music journey includes recording projects such as The Revelation live CD and DVD and The Revival live recording featuring several gospel artists. In addition, he published a personal book titled “A Great Pastor in Church but a Beast to His Family.”

PERSONAL JOURNEY
PERSONAL LIFE

Malusi’s life has also been marked by powerful testimonies of perseverance. In 2014, doctors warned that he might never sing again after discovering a serious voice condition. However, through faith and medical help, he recovered and continued his music ministry.

In September 2022, shortly after hosting a live recording, he faced another serious health challenge and was hospitalized for acute cholecystitis. After several weeks in hospital, he recovered and continues his musical mission.

THE REVIVAL WITH MALUSI MBOKAZI LIVE RECORDING


The 2nd Annual The Revival with Malusi Mbokazi" is a transformative gospel event designed to ignite spiritual renewal and unite people from all walks of life under the powerful motto, “BE REVIVED.” This high energy gathering seeks to create a hullaballoo of worship, combining soul stirring music, dynamic performances, and the Word to inspire deep reflection and revival. The event will proudly showcase exceptional homegrown artists while also inviting renowned gospel superstars to share the stage, fostering unforgettable collaborations and mind provoking performances.

More than just a concert, this experience is a movement a divine call to rejoice, reconnect, and be spiritually awakened. Through electrifying praise, heartfelt testimonies, and communal worship, "The Revival" aims to leave attendees uplifted, empowered, and transformed. With world class production, an inclusive atmosphere, and a mission to spread hope, this annual gathering is set to be a landmark occasion in the gospel community, drawing multitudes to celebrate faith, talent, and the power of revival.
