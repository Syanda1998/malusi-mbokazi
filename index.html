MALUSI MBOKAZI

Malusi Mbokazi is a South African gospel artist, performer, and mentor with a long and impactful career in the gospel music industry. Known for his powerful voice and inspiring ministry, he has performed on many prestigious stages and collaborated with leading gospel artists across Africa.

Throughout his career, Malusi has appeared on major media platforms including interviews on Ukhozi FM, Vuma FM, and Igagasi FM, as well as television appearances on shows such as Adam on Tswane TV.

PERSONAL JOURNEY

Malusi’s life has also been marked by powerful testimonies of perseverance. In 2014, doctors warned that he might never sing again after discovering a serious voice condition. However, through faith and medical help, he recovered and continued his music ministry.

In September 2022, shortly after hosting a live recording, he faced another serious health challenge and was hospitalized for acute cholecystitis. After several weeks in hospital, he recovered and continues his musical mission.
